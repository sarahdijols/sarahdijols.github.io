I am a postdoctoral fellow at UBC . Before, I was a post-doctoral student at Tsinghua University, my mentor was Bin Xu, and then at the MPIM, Bonn, for a few months. I defended my PhD in 2018 (under the supervision of Volker Heiermann) at Aix-Marseille University. I am also a part of the Canadian Automorphic Representations Research Group.

Research

My research interests lie in the interface of representation theory and number theory.
I have been interested in representations of reductive groups over p-adic fields (in particular distinction problems), and automorphic forms.
During my PhD, I proved, in greater generality than previously known, the Generalized Injectivity conjecture of Casselman-Shahidi,
and worked on distinction of unitary groups by their symplectic subgroups.
Since then, I have continued exploring distinction problems (see "Work in Progress" below), and started studying newforms for odd orthogonal groups,
along with Rankin-Selberg L-functions (see the joint work on a G2 L-function).
I also started studying the geometrical and categorical aspects of the local Langlands correspondence,
as part of the aforementioned Canadian Automorphic Representations Research Group.
